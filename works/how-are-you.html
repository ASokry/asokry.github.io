Overview

An interactive website where you can listen to the sound of piano playing as you unwind and relax. Watch the colored dots fall slowly out of view or play along to the rythm of th music and press any computer keys to follow along.

The hardships of life can be quite difficult to bare sometimes. I made this interactive website to help people cope with stress and to relax. Some people relieve stress by listening to music and I find the sound of piano to be quite beautiful and soothing, which is why I choose a particular piano piece. For those who relieve stress through activity, I added a layer of interaction by programming key-presses that will queue visual effects when the dots are on the white line. It's almost like a rythm game, but a much more calming version.


Play and pause the music by clicking on the "PLAY/PAUSE" button on the user interface at the top right corner. You can also change the volume and switch between visual effects for the dots. To see the visual effects, simply press a key when a dot is on the white line. Which the dots fade away, rise, or follow the mouse.

In difficult times, I like to ask myself how I'm doing. It's a nice way of reflecting on one's life, to address your emotions instead of bottling them up, and to help collect one's thoughts when trying to move forward. With that being said, how are you?
