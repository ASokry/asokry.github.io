Online Persona

A project that explores how people associate with their online identities and internet personas to reveal hidden connections within the collective unconscious.Participants complete a survey, answering various questions pertaining to their opinions and views of their online personas. Responses are archived and used to generate real-time data visualizations.

Crab Life: Algae Bandit

Sneak your way through every level to eat all the algae and fight any crabs in the way. In Crab Life, you play as a lone bandit crab who has been rejected by society. Instead of being a decent crab, you take revenge on society by stealing algae from others.
