Sound Ripples:
A P5 sketch that emits a sound and draws an expanding ellipse by clicking on the screen or tapping on a touchscreen. Try it on desktop and moblie.

Try It


Color Ripples:
Similar to Sound Ripples, this P5 sketch primarily focuses on expanding ellipses. The only difference is that they don't disappear unless the page refreshes. This creates colorful interactions between the many growing circles. Try it on desktop and moblie.

Try It


Against the Wall:
The very first video game I ever made. I was first introduced to the GameMaker Studio engine with little to no knowledge of game development. I hit many hurdles in completing this and although it is honestly nothing grand, it was still a worthwhile experience and is personally considered decent for someone first video game. Can only be downloaded on windows. NOTE that the game does not have a quit "button", but can be stopped by closing the window.
