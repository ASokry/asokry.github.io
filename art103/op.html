Oblique Strategies and Process

Description:
Our group name is "Big Ants" and is composed of the following members: Calvin Lee, Monica Munoz, Mateo Campos, and myself. From the Oblique Strategies website, we were given "What are the sections of? Imagine a caterpillar moving", "Ask people to work against their better judgement", and "(Organic) Machinery". For the assigment we chose to do a video collage based on the oblique strategy (Organic) Machinery. We chose this strategy, because to us we saw at being nearly everywhere thus being more open to interpretation. An ant colony for one acts very systematically almost like a well oiled machine. Are bodies are also very mechincal, even though we are organic, if you focus on the process of how we move and even on a molecular level.

We used Instagram's boomerang feature to record clips of everyday life scenarios exhibiting machine-like qualities and/or characteristics.
